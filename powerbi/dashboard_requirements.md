# HR Analytics Dashboard - Power BI Requirements

## Dashboard Overview
Interactive Power BI dashboard for monitoring key HR metrics and employee attrition patterns.

## Key Metrics to Visualize

### 1. Executive Summary Cards
- Total Employees: 1,400+
- Overall Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Average Employee Age: 37 years
- Average Salary: $78,500

### 2. Attrition Analysis
- Department-wise attrition rates
- Gender-based attrition comparison (Female: 14.8%, Male: 17%)
- Technical background impact (24% attrition for technical employees)

### 3. Department Performance
- R&D department: 56% attrition rate (highest)
- Department-wise employee distribution
- Salary distribution by department

### 4. Employee Demographics
- Age distribution across departments
- Gender distribution
- Experience level analysis
- Education background breakdown

### 5. Performance Metrics
- Performance rating distribution
- Work-life balance scores
- Overtime patterns
- Training participation

## Visual Components

### Charts and Graphs
1. **Attrition Rate by Department** - Horizontal bar chart
2. **Gender Attrition Comparison** - Clustered column chart
3. **Technical vs Non-Technical Attrition** - Pie chart
4. **Salary Distribution** - Histogram
5. **Age Distribution** - Histogram
6. **Department Employee Count** - Donut chart
7. **Performance Rating Trends** - Line chart
8. **Work-Life Balance Scores** - Gauge charts

### Filters and Slicers
- Department filter
- Gender filter
- Age range slider
- Salary range slider
- Attrition status filter

### KPIs and Metrics
- Attrition rate percentage
- Average age
- Average salary
- Total employees
- Training hours per employee
